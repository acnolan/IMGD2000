CITIZEN: Pearson, Norman
DATE: April 3, 1984

FIELD REPORT
content:
An agent tailing Mr. Pearson, in
an attempt to determine any ill
intent, was led around the same
block three times, then around
a few sharp curves. The agent
lost Pearson's trail, and
reported finding the following
message on a scrap of paper
where he lost Pearson:

"I see you."
