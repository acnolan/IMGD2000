DATE: April 2, 1984

TRAIL LOG
content:
A field agent was trailing Mr. Eggleston, looking for any signs of suspicious behavior. Mr.Eggleston took a bike and rode to the Department of Motor Vehicles, circling the block for the next half hour. After that, Eggleston biked to a pay phone and made a call, although the agent was unable to overhear or tap the call.
